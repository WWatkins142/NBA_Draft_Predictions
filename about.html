Objective

Create an accurate predictor of whether a 2022 college basketball player is likely to be drafted into the NBA using a subset of their college statistics

Method

Using a Random Forest Classifier which took in 10 statistics from a dataset of college players from 2009-2021 (Games Played, Minutes Percentage, Offensive Rating, True-Shooting Percentage, Assist Percentage, Turnover Percentage, Block Percentage, Points Over Replacement Per Adjusted Game, Adjusted Offensive Efficiency) we trained our model to output draft probabilities for a player from 2022 given those same statistics.
